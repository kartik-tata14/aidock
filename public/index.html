FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

Why should I use the Chrome extension instead of adding tools manually?

The Chrome extension saves you time by auto-detecting the tool's name, description, and even suggesting a category — all with one click. No more switching tabs, copy-pasting URLs, or typing descriptions. Just click "Save to Dock" while you're on the tool's website.

Why can't I see anything on the Social feed yet?

AIDock is a new community! The Social feed shows tools from people you follow. Start by inviting friends with your referral link or search for users by email to follow. As the community grows, you'll discover more tools shared by others.

Is my data private? Can others see my saved tools?

By default, all your saved tools are private — only you can see them. Tools only become visible to others when you add them to a Stack and share that Stack publicly. You're always in control of what you share.

How do I get more tool slots on the Free tier?

Free accounts start with 10 tool slots. Invite friends using your referral link and earn +2 slots for each friend who joins (up to 5 friends = +10 bonus slots). Want unlimited? Upgrade to Pro for just $4.99/month (early-bird pricing).

What's the difference between Tools and Stacks?

Tools are individual AI products you save to your dashboard (like ChatGPT, Midjourney, etc.). Stacks are curated collections of tools grouped by theme — like "My Content Creation Stack" or "Developer Essentials." Stacks are perfect for sharing your toolkit with others.

Can I use AIDock on multiple devices?

Yes! AIDock is web-based, so your dashboard syncs across all your devices. Just log in from any browser. The Chrome extension works on any desktop Chrome browser where you install it.
